My Portfolio

Realtor Assist

I created Realtor Assist for my final project of my Bootcamp. I decided to work by myself because I wanted to create an app that would help me at my job as a Real Estate assistant. Realtor Assist is made with Javascript, React, Express, Mongodb, Node.js and Bootstrap.

Primp & Play

For the second project in my bootcamp I worked on Primp and play with a group of three other women. We decided to make a pet grooming and bathing website. I worked on the front end by styling the home page and the new member sign in form. The technologies we used to build Primp & Play are HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Sequelize, MySQL, Node.js and Express. This project taught me how to create and style forms as well as how to work with the back end team to link everything to the database to store the information inputted on the forms.

Giftastic-The Office

Giftastic was a fun bootcamp homework built using Gifs. I am a big fan of The Office so I chose to use gifs of the characters. The GifTastic application searches the GIPHY API to find gifs by pressing search buttons. There is a selection of buttons that are pre-defined, but users can create other buttons through the form on the page. Gifs are displayed with their rating and can be played by pressing them. I built Giftastic with HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, and the Giphy API.
